OUR STORY

MADE WITH FUEGO

"The food is delicious! Jennifer is the nicest person ever. Highly recommend the chicharrón mofongo and tres golpes."

— MARINA SABILLÓN, GOOGLE REVIEW

El Buen Sazón is a family restaurant rooted in the flavors of the Dominican Republic and Latin Caribbean tradition. Every dish is made with real ingredients, real care, and real sazón — that unmistakable seasoning that makes Latin food unlike anything else.

Located on Chestnut Street in Reading, PA, we serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner with the warmth of a home kitchen. From our signature chicharrón mofongo to our tres golpes and fresh-squeezed juices, every plate is made to satisfy.

Free delivery available. Come hungry, leave happy.
